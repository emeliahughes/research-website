ARUW

Advanced Robotics at the University of Washington

One of the flagstone experiences of my time while at UW was being a part of ARUW. I joined the team in my freshman year, then led as Vice President for two years before becoming the President in my final year. So much happened over this period that I can't fit it into one page, however I want to highlight some of the achievements my team and I made.

President: 2021-2022
Vice President: 2020-2021, 2019-2020
Member: 2018-2019
