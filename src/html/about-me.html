Emelia May Hughes

Hello! I’m Emelia ⭐ Pleasure to meet you.

I just graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Science in Informatics and a Bachelor of Arts in Drawing & Painting. My research focuses on accessible technology, social computing, and online communities. I am also currently in the process of applying to PhD programs.

I am currently working with Amy Zhang at the Social Futures Lab. Right now, my work focuses on credibility and misinformation on social media. For more info, see Project Viblio.

Outside of work, I love figure skating (I used to skate competitively), exploring the world (when COVID-19 permits, of course), and slowly creating my own urban jungle (also known as fueling my plant addiction).

Right now, I am focusing on my research and am exploring my art in my free time. While I am not pursuing art as a career right now, I am still determined to make it a part of my life. If you are interested in working with me on something art related, feel free to reach out!

I do admit I have one (or five) too many hobbies, but I also love to casually research dress history, knit, sew, listen to true-crime podcasts, and hang out at my local Pure Barre.
