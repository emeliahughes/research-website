Painting Thesis

About project

To complete my painting & drawing degree, I explored my painting through the following thesis project. I pushed myself out of my comfort zone and spent a lot of time painting in new ways.

Research

I began by researching both historical and contemporary artists. I used their work as inspiration as well as a reference for technique. I've included some of my favorite pieces below.

Development

To explore different compositions and topics, I spent time doing many quick studies with a limited color palette.

I also had the chance to implement this practice with a live figure model. I did two quick studies, then a (relatively) quick painting.

Practice

Next, I moved on to iterating on my ideas for my final paintings. I used multiple mediums and compositions to figure out the best direction.

Implement

Finally, I worked on the following series. The three paintings were completed over a period of about 6 months.

Bonus: Exhibit!

At the end of the year, I had the wonderful opportunity to exhibit my work alongside other students of the School of Art Honors Cohort at the Jacob Lawrence Gallery.
